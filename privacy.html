Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2025

CasaLink is committed to safeguarding your privacy in accordance with Zambia’s Data Protection Act and other relevant laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information including your name, contact details (e.g., phone number, email), and payment information when you register, list a property, or book a rental. We also gather app usage data to enhance functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Facilitate property listings, bookings, and communication between landlords and tenants
  • Process payments securely
  • Improve CasaLink’s services and user experience
  • Comply with Zambian legal requirements

3. Data Protection

We employ reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, in line with Zambian data protection standards. However, no system is entirely immune to risks, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by Zambian law (e.g., court orders) or to provide our services (e.g., payment processors). We do not sell or trade your data.

5. Your Rights

Under Zambian law, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@casalink.homes.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy to reflect legal updates or changes in our practices. Users will be informed of updates, and continued use of CasaLink signifies acceptance of the revised policy.
